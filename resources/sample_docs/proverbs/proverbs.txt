"Time and tide wait for no man."

This proverb emphasizes the inevitability of time's passage, reminding us that we must make the most of our opportunities as time will not pause for anyone.

"The best time to plant a tree was twenty years ago. The second best time is now."

This proverb highlights the importance of taking action without delay. It suggests that while starting early is ideal, it is never too late to begin working towards your goals.

"Time heals all wounds."

This proverb conveys the idea that with the passage of time, emotional pain and difficulties will lessen. It offers a comforting perspective on the healing process.
